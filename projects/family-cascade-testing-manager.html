Case Study

Family Cascade Testing Manager

A workflow-focused platform for genetic counseling teams to coordinate family outreach, track cascade testing status, and improve completion rates.

Problem

At-risk relatives often never complete testing because outreach and follow-up coordination are fragmented across spreadsheets, calls, and manual reminders.

What Was Built

  • Family testing workflow with role-aware status progression
  • Coordination dashboard for outreach and completion tracking
  • Core screens for roster, actions, and timeline visibility
  • Tested frontend foundation ready for deeper healthcare workflow logic

Current Status

  • Project deployed to Vercel production
  • Lint and build passing in local validation
  • Ready for iteration on compliance-aware data and notification flows
