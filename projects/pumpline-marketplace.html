Case Study

Pumpline Marketplace

A trust-first septic services marketplace designed for rural homeowners and local providers.

Problem

Septic emergencies are high-stakes and expensive, but homeowners often rely on unreliable word-of-mouth with weak trust signals, delayed response times, and inconsistent pricing transparency.

Build Scope

  • County-first provider directory with profile quality and trust signals
  • Provider pages showing response expectations, pricing ranges, and completed-work artifacts
  • Review and reputation model focused on verified local service outcomes
  • Pilot-friendly onboarding flow for trusted local providers
  • Foundation for property-level maintenance history as a phase-two data moat

Tech Stack

React, TypeScript, Vite, custom CSS, Vitest, Vercel

Outcome

MVP is live with core marketplace flows and supporting test/lint/build quality gates. The product is structured for county-by-county rollout and phased trust/data expansion.
