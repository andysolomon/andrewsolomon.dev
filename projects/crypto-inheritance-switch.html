Case Study

Crypto Inheritance Switch

A crypto inheritance workflow app for configuring beneficiaries, trigger rules, and guided recovery instructions for non-technical family members.

Problem

Many holders postpone inheritance planning because existing options are complex, technical, and difficult for beneficiaries to execute under stress.

What Was Built

  • Complete route flow: setup, beneficiaries, trigger rules, recovery guides, timeline, and settings
  • Beneficiary management flow with verification state transitions
  • Trigger stage progression with check-in, reminder, and activation controls
  • Dedicated claim intake flow and beneficiary portal views
  • Legal checklist workflow for evidence readiness and review gating
  • Timeline model that records workflow events across critical actions

Current Status

  • Project pushed to GitHub
  • Deployed to Vercel production
  • Expanded workflow pages shipped and integrated into navigation
  • E2E coverage added for claim flow, beneficiary portal, legal checklist, and settings import
